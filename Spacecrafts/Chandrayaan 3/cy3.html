Propulsion Module

The propulsion module is an essential component of the Chandrayaan-3 mission. It provides the necessary thrust for the spacecraft to reach the Moon's orbit. The propulsion system consists of advanced engines and propellant tanks, ensuring precise control and maneuverability during the mission.
