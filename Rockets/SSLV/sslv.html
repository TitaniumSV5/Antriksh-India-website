Basic Information

  • 3-stage launch vehicle

  • Payload capacity:

    500 kg to LEO

  • Height of launch vehicle: 34 m

  • Max Diameter of launch vehicle: 2 m

  • First launch on: 7 August 2022

  • Total launches: 2 (1 successes and 1 failures)
