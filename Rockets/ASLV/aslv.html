ASLV

Augmented Satellite Launch Vehicle

Basic Information

  • 5-stage launch vehicle

  • Payload capacity :

    150 kg to LEO

  • Mass of launch vehicle : 41000 kg

  • Height of launch vehicle: 23.5m

  • Max Diameter of launch vehicle: 1m

  • First launch on: 24 March 1987

  • Total launches: 4 (1 successes, 2 failures and 1 partial failure)

Stage Information

First stage/Side Boosters :

- 2 Solid Rocket Motor
- Propellant - PBAN (Polybutadiene Acrylonitrile)
- Thrust - 502.6kN each
- Specific Impulse (Isp) - 253 sec
- Burn time - 49 sec

Second stage/Core :

- Solid Rocket Motor
- Propellant - PBAN (Polybutadiene Acrylonitrile)
- Thrust - 702.6kN
- Specific Impulse (Isp) - 259 sec
- Burn time - 45 sec

Third stage :

- Solid Rocket Motor
- Propellants - PBAN (Polybutadiene Acrylonitrile)
- Thrust - 304kN
- Specific Impulse (Isp) - 276 sec
- Burn time - 36 sec
- Propellant mass: 42000 kg

Fourth stage:

- Solid Rocket Motor
- Propellant - HEF20
- Thrust - 90.7kN
- Specific Impulse (Isp) - 277 sec
- Burn time - 45 sec

Fifth stage:

- Solid Rocket Motor
- Propellants - HEF20
- Thrust - 35kN
- Specific Impulse (Isp) - 281 sec
- Burn time - 33 sec
